Card Crunch Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Card Crunch stores game progress and settings on your device. The game does not require a Card Crunch account and does not sell personal information.

Data stored on your device

Scores, pot progress, settings, and resumable run data are saved locally so the game can continue between sessions. You can erase this data with Reset Save Data in Settings or by clearing the app's storage.

Google Play Games Services

On Android, Card Crunch may use Google Play Games Services for platform sign-in and leaderboards. Google processes this data under the Google Privacy Policy. Card Crunch submits only eligible gameplay scores to the configured leaderboard.

Advertising

The current release does not enable a production advertising provider. If advertising is enabled in a future release, this policy and the Google Play Data safety disclosure will be updated before release.

Children

Card Crunch does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support address listed on the Card Crunch Google Play store page.

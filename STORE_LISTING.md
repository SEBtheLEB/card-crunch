## Full description

Pick the cards you think connect, slam CRUNCH, and watch the table come alive.

Card Crunch is a fast portrait card game built around bold decisions and bigger score explosions. Match suits and numbers, complete plus and minus Crunches, chain multiple cards in one move, and bank your winnings before the run falls apart.

Build a multiplier with every successful Crunch. Push your luck for a larger payout, or bank early to make permanent progress toward the next pot. Three lives stand between a fresh run and losing everything you left unbanked.

Features:

- Fast, one-thumb portrait play
- Suit, number, plus, and minus Crunches
- Multi-card combos and dramatic result cut-ins
- Push-your-luck banking and rising multipliers
- Persistent pots and local progress
- Google Play Games leaderboards
- Offline-ready core gameplay
- Optional sound, music, and reduced-motion settings

How far can you push the table before you bank?
